Why I Like Golf

Golf is one of my favorite things to do with family and friends. It’s competitive but also relaxing, and it feels good when you start to see little improvements over time. This is a picture of a golf course in Vietnam that I hope to play one day. As I served my mission there, I would always ask people if they had played here!

Practice Plan

Here is a simple practice routine I like. This also shows the required list inside a list for the assignment.

  1. Warm up
    • Ten wedge shots
    • Ten iron shots
    • Ten drives focusing on tempo
  2. Short game
    • Chip from different lies around the green
    • Lag putts from about thirty to forty feet
    • Ten putts inside four feet
  3. Play a few holes and work on course management
